CTRL+ALT+CROCHET

Along with creating digital art and enamel pins (often inspired by retro tech / UI and 1980s-1990s nostalgia with a surreal, dreamy overlay), my main passion is fiber art, particularly crochet. My inspiration from retro tech also comes out in my crochet projects sometimes. I love making silly and niche art pieces using bold color schemes while fusing in memes, nerdy references, nods to early internet culture, and other counterculture icons.

I really enjoy making blankets and bags, but sweaters and other wearables are my favorite. I feel like coding and crochet go hand in hand; they require the same type of problem solving and deciphering a crochet pattern is very akin to writing code in my opinion!
